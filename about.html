History About University Of People

UOP is the university built in 1999 using the force of people for their relatives either children, families and other people around the world.

by the help of University Of Campbel in the same country this UOP started with great force from the library and other materials from that campus.

The Government leadership did all they can for the campus to work as well as to help people who are unable to complete their studies, but the they come to accomplish what people has started.

The campus has started with 4 faculties that are :

  1. Bachelor of Business Administration with :
    • BBA in Finance
    • BBA in Econnomics
    • BBA in BIT
  2. Bachelor in Computer Science
  3. Bachelor in Applied Stitistics
  4. Bachelor in Mining and geology
